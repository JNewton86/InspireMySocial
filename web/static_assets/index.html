FAQ Section

InspireMySocial.com is an AI-powered platform designed to help individuals and businesses effortlessly create their social media content. We understand the common challenges faced by users, such as limited time to create content, lack of inspiration, and difficulty in navigating multiple social networks. Our AI-driven solution addresses these issues by providing tailored content suggestions, expediting content delivery across multiple platforms, and utilizing the unique benefits of each social media channel. Our aim is to help you inspire, engage, and grow your audience so you can focus on what matters most to you.

The Free package includes the Facebook Post Module and 5 monthly credits. Starter, Pro, and Socialite packages unlock all content modules for Facebook, Instagram, YouTube Short, YouTube Long Video, Twitter, and LinkedIn. The key difference is the number of monthly credits offered: 20 for Starter, 45 for Pro, and 75 for Socialite. To determine the best package for you, assess your content requirements and desired frequency of postings on each platform. The more content you need, the higher the package tier you should choose, ensuring you have enough credits to fulfill your social media goals.

Monthly credits are used to generate content across all supported social media platforms. Each content piece, whether a post or video, consumes one credit. Please note that the Youtube Long Script consumes two credits per use. Keep in mind that unused credits do not carry over to the next month, so it's essential to plan your content usage according to your package's credit allotment to maximize the benefits.
